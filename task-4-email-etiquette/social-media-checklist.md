# Social Media Checklist: The Do’s

- [ ] **Audit Digital Footprint** Search your name every month to understand what a recruiter or professor will find when they search for you.
  
- [ ] **Enable 2FA** Secure your accounts on all social networking sites. As a CSE Core student, you know how easily accounts can be hacked. Remember to keep your keep 2FA active on every single platform.
  
- [ ] **Fact Check Before Sharing** Before pressing "repost" on campus safety alerts or news stories, confirm the source to avoid spreading false information.
  
- [ ] **Network Intentionally** Use LinkedIn to connect with industry experts in your degree (B.Tech - Computer Science Engineering). Make sure to use this tool to create relationships with VIT alumni and professionals in industry.
  
- [ ] **Professional Bio** Keep your profile, bio up to date and reflecting your status as a student of your prestigious college.

# The Don’ts

- [ ] **Share Too Much Personal Information:** Do not share photos of your University-issued ID, your International students’ hostel number, or images with a screenshot showing your OTP.
- [ ] **Feed Into The Trolls:** Do not be involved in vociferous public debates or leave any comments in toxic and incendiary Dialogue threads that could hurt or tarnish your reputation.
- [ ] **Post Without Getting Permission:** Do not tag friends or publish photos of other students on campus without receiving permission from those you are tagging or photographing. Be considerate of other's digital privacy wishes.
- [ ] **Disregard Your Privacy Settings:** Do not leave your personal accounts open as "Public" by default. Always review what your audience settings are.
- [ ] **Plagiarize Content:** Never copy someone else's images, digital artwork, or code snippets without giving credit to the original creator.
